Frequently Asked Questions

What is Choghadiya?

Choghadiya is a system of time division in Vedic Astrology used to find auspicious times during the day and night. It consists of seven types of periods: Amrit, Shubh, Labh, Chara, Rog, Kaal, and Udveg.

How accurate is the online birth chart tool?

Our tool uses simplified astronomical algorithms to calculate your Moon sign and Nakshatra. For a complete and precise Horoscope Reading, professional software and manual analysis by Dr. Dhaval Trivedi are recommended.

Can I use these tools for marriage matching?

You can use the 'Sign & Nakshatra' tool to check Yoni and Gana, which are key factors in Gunamilan (Compatibility Matching). However, a full Kundali Match requires deeper analysis.

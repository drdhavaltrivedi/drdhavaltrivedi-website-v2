Free online palm reading tool: try it first

By Dr. Dhaval Trivedi · Feb 2025

We run a free palm reading platform that uses AI to read a photo of your palm. You upload a clear image, and you get instant feedback on lines, mounts, and basic traits. No signup, no payment. It is a good way to see whether palmistry resonates with you before booking a full session.

The tool lives at palmistry.appstream.me. Use a well-lit photo of your palm (both hands if you can), and keep the lines as visible as possible. The AI looks at the same features a practitioner would: heart line, head line, life line, and the mounts under each finger. What you get is a first pass, not a full reading.

When does it make sense to book a real session? When you want to go deeper: specific questions about career, relationships, or timing, or when you want both hands read together with context. I also look at minor lines and markings that the tool does not cover. So use the free tool to play and learn; use a palmistry consultation when you need clarity you can act on.

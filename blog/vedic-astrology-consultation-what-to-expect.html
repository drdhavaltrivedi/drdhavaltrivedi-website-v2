What to expect in a Vedic Astrology consultation

By Dr. Dhaval Trivedi · Feb 2025

Booking your first Jyotish session can feel unclear if you do not know what happens in the room. Here is a straight breakdown from practice: what we use, what you need to bring, and what you can ask.

Every reading starts with your birth details: date, time, and place. Accuracy matters. Even a rough time (within an hour) helps. From that we cast your birth chart (Kundli) and look at the positions of the nine grahas, the twelve houses, and the nakshatras. That chart is the basis of the session.

In the consultation we typically cover: your core tendencies (from the chart), current and upcoming planetary periods (Dashas), and transits that are affecting you now. You can ask about career, relationships, health, timing of a decision, or a general life theme. I do not predict fixed events. I outline possibilities, strengths, and timings so you can make better choices.

Come with one or two clear questions. It helps to focus the reading. If you have already tried our other services, such as numerology or palmistry, you can connect those insights in the same conversation.
